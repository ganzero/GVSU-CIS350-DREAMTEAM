# Crown Use-Case Description

## Use Case
Crown piece

## Actor(s)
Player 1 or player 2

## Description
The player arrives at the opposite end of the board relative to their starting point. 
The player is crowned with a different symbol than the blank, colored pieces. 
The player can now move backwards and forwards diagonally. 
On completion, the player may move the crowned piece or another on their next turn.
